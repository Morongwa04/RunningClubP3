About Our Club

Our running club was founded 2025, by a solo runner and now it has grown to a community of active members.

Our Running Club brings together runners of all levels to promote health, fitness, and community spirit. We host weekly runs, marathons, and training programs for everyone.

Our mission

Our misson is to promote running as a way to improve physical and mental wellbeing while building meaningful connections.

What we believe

We believe that running is for everyone , regardless of speed or experience.Our inclusive approach ensures that every member feels supported and encouraged to reach their personal goals.
